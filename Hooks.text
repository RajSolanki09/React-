useState - state manage karne k liye..

useEffect - side effects handle karne k liye (jaise API call, DOM manipulation,event listener).

useContext - globle state ko consume karne k liye without propdrilling.

useReducer - complex state management k liye (redux jaisa chhota version).

useRef - mutable values hold karne k liye jo rerender trigger na karein , ya DOM access karne k liye.

useMemo & useCallback - optimization k liye , unnecessary re-renders avoid karne k liye.
